About Us

Precision 3D Printing • Engineering • Prototyping

Print Sleep Repeat is a specialist 3D-printing service providing FDM printing, CAD modelling, prototyping, and short-run manufacturing for hobbyists, engineers, creators, and businesses, our main specialty is in collectable music album covers, movie posters, band merch & horror related wall art.

Whether you need a replacement part, a fully custom model, a functional prototype, or a batch of production-quality prints, we deliver high accuracy and fast turnaround.

With experience in mechanical design, multi-material printing, and precision resin work, we help ideas become real-world objects — fast, clean, and cost-effective.
